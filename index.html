Popular Destinations In Egypt

Nestled along the Red Sea, Sahl Hasheesh is a world-class resort destination that boasts a perfect blend of luxury and natural beauty. This destination is graced with breathtaking sandy beaches, turquoise waters, and picturesque landscapes, making it an idyllic holiday spot for travelers worldwide. Sahl Hasheesh is a paradise for divers and snorkelers who want to explore the mesmerizing underwater world of the Red Sea.

The city of Marsa Alam is a jewel among Egyptian tourist destinations. This quiet coastal town offers the best of both worlds — a perfect respite from the crowded streets of Cairo, as well as sunny beaches and pristine coral reefs for active holidaymakers wishing to experience scuba diving, snorkeling, and sailing. The city’s coastline runs 217 kilometers. Due to its crystal clear water and white sandy beaches, it is also known as the “Egyptian Maldives”.

Sharm El Sheikh is the Sinai Peninsula’s major tourism Centre and one of the world’s top diving destinations. It is the underwater wonders of the Red Sea – particularly the waters of the Ras Muhammed Marine Park just south of town – that put the city Centre stage in the first place, and the diving and snorkeling offered here continue to attract flocks of diving enthusiasts each year. A city of contrasts. It is home to incredible snorkeling and diving by day but also boasts a thriving night-life with shopping streets, café and restaurants, and clubs in its famous Naama Bay.
